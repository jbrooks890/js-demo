My Favorite Things

My favorite color is red. My favorite superhero is Batman.
Below are some of my favorite hobbies.

Writing

I like to write stories. In fact, I self-published a Fantasy novel called Arcan: The Missing Nexus in 2016. Writing is therapeudic for me. I enjoy telling stories, even if I never share them with anyone. I'm writing this right now to share with you. You're welcome.

Drawing

I draw a lot, but mostly for the many, many projects I'm working on. I do more digital art noawadays, but still am fond of doodling in my notebooks. I like to draw Fantasy and Sci-Fi characters and also logos and icons.

Movies

Going out usually involves food and a movie— simultaneously when possible. I like most genres that are not pure Romance (romcoms are cool). But my main preference is Fantasy/Sci-Fi and Action. I have many favorites, though I'm not sure what movie tops the list. The Matrix is one of them.

Games

I enjoy making them. I enjoy playing them too, but it's rare that I have time. My favorite genres are: adventure, platforming, fighting, RPG and racing. I want to play Elden Ring but refuse to play it on anything other than a PlayStation 5.
